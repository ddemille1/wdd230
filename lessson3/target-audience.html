Marietta Ohio Chamber of Commerce
Website Planning Document

Target audience:

The target audience of the Marietta Ohio Chamber of Commerce website is business owners in the city of Marietta, Ohio, and the surrounding area. They are interested in building their businesses by networking with other business owners in the area. They are seeking to prompte their businesses by being affiliated with the chamber. Their age ranges from 18 to 80 yrs old. They have various levels of business experience, success, and income. Their businesses range from small mom and pop stores to medium sized corporations.
