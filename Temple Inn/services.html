Services

Services

From our wedding reception hall to our family search center we can meet the unique needs of temple workers and patrons.

Reception Hall

Getting sealed in the temple is one of the most important days in a latter-day saint's life. We are here to help make the day as smooth as possible. Each of our inns have reception halls conveniently located near the temples

Missionary Suites

If you are a full-time temple missionary we are here to help with your housing needs. We offer extended stay suites at affordable prices so you can more effectively do the work of the Lord.

Family Search

Do you need to do a bit of family history before or after your temple session? We have all the resources you need to get your family names ready for the temple.
