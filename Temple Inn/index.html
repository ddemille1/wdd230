Temple Spotlight

The Gilbert Arizona temple has a special place in our company's history. The first Temple Inn and Suites was built next door to this temple in 2015.

The Gilbert Arizona temple was announced in 2008 by President Thomas S. Monson. It was one of the first temples announced during his presidency. The temple was dedicated in 2014. It is the 142nd temple built by the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day saints and the 4th in Arizona. It stands at 195 feet tall and is 85,326 square feet. The exterior is build of precast concrete and white quartz.
