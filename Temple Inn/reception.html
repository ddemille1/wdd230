Receptions

Accommodations

Each of our Inns features a reception hall that can accommodate up to 300 guests. Some locations also offer outdoor seating.

Catering

Our in-house catering staff is well trained and professional. Each of our locations employes a team of highly trained chefs. We offer many types of cuisine such as Mexican, Italian, Greek, Tai and American. If you can eat it we can make it!

Setup

Our reception halls can be decorated in which ever way fits you best. Pick a theme and we'll do all the setup and clean up. Packages include everything you need to make your day special.
