News Release

The Harmar Bridge Company announced today that it will be completeing the first phase of the the Harmar Bridge restoration. The company has been fundraising since the close of the bridge five years ago. Don Sweeney, presidend of the Harmar Bridge Company said they have now raised enough money to begin the pier inspections which is the first stage of renovating the bridge.
